1. Calculate the area of the longer walls:  
   \( 2 \times (14 \, \text{ft} \times 8.5 \, \text{ft}) = 2 \times 119 \, \text{sq ft} = 238 \, \text{sq ft} \).

2. Calculate the area of the shorter walls:  
   \( 2 \times (10 \, \text{ft} \times 8.5 \, \text{ft}) = 2 \times 85 \, \text{sq ft} = 170 \, \text{sq ft} \).

3. Add the areas to get the total wall area:  
   \( 238 \, \text{sq ft} + 170 \, \text{sq ft} = 408 \, \text{sq ft} \).

\( \frac{408}{36} = 11.333 \, \text{quarts} \).

\( 12 \times 14.80 = 177.60 \).
