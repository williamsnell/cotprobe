### Key Steps and Analysis

1. **Conservation of Momentum**:
   - Before the collision, the total momentum is \( m_1 u_1 + m_2 u_2 \).
   - After the collision, \( m_1 \) is at rest, so the total momentum is \( m_2 v_2 \).
   - Therefore, \( m_1 u_1 + m_2 u_2 = m_2 v_2 \).

2. **Conservation of Kinetic Energy**:
   - Before the collision, the total kinetic energy is \( \frac{1}{2} m_1 u_1^2 + \frac{1}{2} m_2 u_2^2 \).
   - After the collision, \( m_1 \) is at rest, so the total kinetic energy is \( \frac{1}{2} m_2 v_2^2 \).
   - Therefore, \( \frac{1}{2} m_1 u_1^2 + \frac{1}{2} m_2 u_2^2 = \frac{1}{2} m_2 v_2^2 \).

   - After the collision, the velocities are given by:
     \[
     v_1 = \frac{m_1 - m_2}{m_1 + m_2} u_1 + \frac{2 m_2}{m_1 + m_2} u_2

     v_2 = \frac{2 m_1}{m_1 + m_2} u_1 + \frac{m_2 - m_1}{m_1 + m_2} u_2
