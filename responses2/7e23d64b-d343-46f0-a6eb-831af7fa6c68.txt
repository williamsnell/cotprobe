2. **Net National Product (NNP):** It is the total value of final goods and services produced by a country's residents during a specific period, minus depreciation of fixed capital. NNP represents the net output of the economy after accounting for the wear and tear of assets.

From these definitions, we can establish the relationship:
   - NNP = GNP - Depreciation
   - This means that GNP is equal to NNP plus the depreciation cost. Therefore, GNP = NNP + Depreciation.
