\[ \text{NH4NO3} \rightarrow \text{N2O} + 2\text{H2O} \]

\[ 900 \, \text{g} / 80 \, \text{g/mol} = 11.25 \, \text{moles} \]

\[ V = nRT/P \]

- \( T = 1092 \, \text{K} \) (but this is not possible as 1092 K is unrealistic for the given conditions)
