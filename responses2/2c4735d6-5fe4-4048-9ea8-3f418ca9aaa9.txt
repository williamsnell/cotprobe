\]

The solubility product constant (\(K_{sp}\)) is given by:

\[
K_{sp} = [\text{Fe}^{3+}][\text{OH}^-]^3 = s (3s)^3 = 27 s^4
\]

Rearranging for \(s\):

\[
s^4 = \frac{K_{sp}}{27} = \frac{1.6 \times 10^{-39}}{27} \approx 5.9259 \times 10^{-40}
\]

Taking the fourth root:

\[
s = (5.9259 \times 10^{-40})^{1/4} \approx 1.55 \times 10^{-10} \, \text{M}
\]

Thus, the molar solubility of Fe(OH)₃ is approximately \(1.55 \times 10^{-10} \, \text{M}\).
