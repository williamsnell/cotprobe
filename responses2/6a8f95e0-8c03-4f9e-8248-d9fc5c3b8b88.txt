\[
\text{Re} = \frac{u \cdot L}{\nu}
\]

where \(u\) is the flow velocity, \(L\) is the characteristic length, and \(\nu\) is the kinematic viscosity. For dynamic similarity, the Reynolds number for the model (\(\text{Re}_\text{model}\)) must equal that for the actual system (\(\text{Re}_\text{tunnel}\)):

\[
\text{Re}_\text{model} = \text{Re}_\text{tunnel}
\]

\[
\frac{u' \cdot L'}{\nu} = \frac{U \cdot L}{\nu}
