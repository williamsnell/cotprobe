1. **Find the mean (average) of the data:**
   \[
   \text{Mean} = \frac{5 + 8 + 2 + 1}{4} = \frac{16}{4} = 4
   \]

   \[
   (5 - 4)^2 = 1^2 = 1 \\
   (8 - 4)^2 = 4^2 = 16 \\
   (2 - 4)^2 = (-2)^2 = 4 \\
   (1 - 4)^2 = (-3)^2 = 9
   \]

3. **Sum the squared deviations:**
   \[
   1 + 16 + 4 + 9 = 30
   \]

4. **Divide the sum by the sample size minus one (n-1) to find the variance:**
   \[
   \text{Variance} = \frac{30}{4 - 1} = \frac{30}{3} = 10
   \]

5. **Take the square root of the variance to find the standard deviation:**
   \[
   \text{Standard Deviation} = \sqrt{10} \approx 3.16
