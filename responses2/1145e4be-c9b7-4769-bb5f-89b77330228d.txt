The problem involves an object placed 1 foot (12 inches) from a concave mirror with a radius of 4 feet (focal length \( f = 24 \, \text{inches} \)). The object is moved 1 inch closer to the mirror, and the corresponding displacement of the image is to be determined.

### Step 1: Mirror Formula
The mirror equation is:

\[
\frac{1}{f} = \frac{1}{u} + \frac{1}{v}
\]

where:
- \( u = 12 \, \text{inches} \) (initial object distance),
- \( f = 24 \, \text{inches} \) (focal length),
- \( v \) is the image distance.

Rearranging for \( v \):

\[
\frac{1}{v} = \frac{1}{f} - \frac{1}{u}
\]

Substituting the values:

\[
\frac{1}{v} = \frac{1}{24} - \frac{1}{12} = \frac{1}{24} - \frac{2}{24} = -\frac{1}{24}
\]

Thus:

\[
v = -24 \, \text{inches}
\]

The image is virtual and located 24 inches behind the mirror.

### Step 2: New Image Distance
The object is moved 1 inch closer to the mirror, so the new object distance is:

\[
u' = 12 - 1 = 11 \, \text{inches}
\]

Using the mirror formula again to find the new image distance \( v' \):

\[
\frac{1}{f} = \frac{1}{u'} + \frac{1}{v'}
\]

Substituting the values:

\[
\frac{1}{24} = \frac{1}{11} + \frac{1}{v'}
\]

Solving for \( \frac{1}{v'} \):

\[
\frac{1}{v'} = \frac{1}{24} - \frac{1}{11} = \frac{11 - 24}{24 \cdot 11} = \frac{-13}{264}
\]

Thus:

\[
v' = -\frac{264}{13} \approx -20.3077 \, \text{inches}
\]

### Step 3: Displacement of the Image
The initial image position is \( v = -24 \, \text{inches} \), and the new image position is \( v' = -20.3077 \, \text{inches} \). The displacement of the image is the change in its position:

\[
\Delta v = v' - v = (-20.3077) - (-24) = 3.6923 \, \text{inches}
\]

### Step 4: Interpretation of the Displacement
The displacement of the image is approximately \( 3.7 \, \text{inches} \), and since the image moves further away from the mirror as the object moves closer, the displacement is in the same direction as the object's movement.

### Final Answer
The image moves \( 3.7 \, \text{inches} \) away from the mirror. This corresponds to option **F**.
