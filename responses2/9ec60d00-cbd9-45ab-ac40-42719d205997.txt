From the problem statement, each allele (A or B) contributes 5 cm to the base height. The base height is 5 cm. Each plant's height is calculated as the base height plus the contributions from all alleles. 

For a genotype with two A's and two B's (AABB), the contributions are:
- Two A's contribute \(2 \times 5 = 10\) cm.
- Two B's contribute \(2 \times 5 = 10\) cm.

Total height = Base (5 cm) + Contributions (10 cm + 10 cm) = 25 cm.

- Two a's contribute \(2 \times 5 = 10\) cm.
- Two b's contribute \(2 \times 5 = 10\) cm.

Total height = Base (5 cm) + Contributions (10 cm + 10 cm) = 25 cm.
