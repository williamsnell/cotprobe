where \(X_{\text{BA}}\) is the mole fraction of benzoic acid, \(P_{\text{BA}}^0 = 0.00405 \, \text{atm}\), \(P_{\text{EtOH}}^0 = 1 \, \text{atm}\), and \(X_{\text{EtOH}} = 1 - X_{\text{BA}}\).
