\[
\text{Re} = \frac{V \cdot L}{\nu}
\]

where \( V \) is the velocity, \( L \) is the characteristic length (diameter or length), and \( \nu \) is the kinematic viscosity of water.

\[
\frac{V_p \cdot L_p}{\nu} = \frac{V_m \cdot L_m}{\nu}
