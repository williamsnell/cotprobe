In the model:
- The wavelength scales as \( \lambda_{\text{model}} = L \cdot \lambda_{\text{prototype}} \), where \( L = 1/25 \).
- The wave speed scales as \( c_{\text{model}} = \sqrt{L} \cdot c_{\text{prototype}} \).

Thus, the model period is:
\[
T_{\text{model}} = \frac{\lambda_{\text{model}}}{c_{\text{model}}} = \frac{L \cdot \lambda_{\text{prototype}}}{\sqrt{L} \cdot c_{\text{prototype}}} = \sqrt{L} \cdot T_{\text{prototype}}.
\]
Substituting \( L = 1/25 \) and \( T_{\text{prototype}} = 10 \, \text{sec} \):
\[
T_{\text{model}} = \sqrt{\frac{1}{25}} \cdot 10 = \frac{1}{5} \cdot 10 = 2 \, \text{sec}.
