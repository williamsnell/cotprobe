**H₂ ⇌ 2H**.

**0.67 moles (H₂) + 0.66 moles (H) = 1.33 moles**.  

Using the ideal gas law, **PV = nRT**, the total pressure is 1 atm, and the temperature is 2000°C, which is 2273 K. The gas constant **R** is 0.0821 L·atm/(mol·K).  

The density **d** is given by:  
\[
d = \frac{\text{mass}}{\text{volume}} = \frac{PM}{RT}
