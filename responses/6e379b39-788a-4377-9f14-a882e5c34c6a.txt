\[
P = F \cdot v
\]

- \( F = 3000 \, \text{lb} \) (thrust),
- \( v = 800 \, \text{ft/s} \) (velocity).

### Step 1: Calculate Power in ft-lbf/s
\[
P = 3000 \cdot 800 = 2,400,000 \, \text{ft-lbf/s}
\]

1 horsepower (hp) is equivalent to \( 550 \, \text{ft-lbf/s} \). Thus:
\[
P_{\text{hp}} = \frac{2,400,000}{550} \approx 4363.64 \, \text{hp}
