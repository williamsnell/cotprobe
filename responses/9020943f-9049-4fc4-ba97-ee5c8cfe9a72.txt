\[
\frac{1}{f} = \frac{1}{v} + \frac{1}{u}
\]

Where:
- \( f \) is the focal length of the lens (in meters),
- \( u \) is the object distance from the lens (in meters),
- \( v \) is the image distance from the lens (in meters).

1. The student's glasses are 1.5 cm from his eyes. The book is 20 cm from his eyes, so the object distance from the lens is:
   \[
   u = 20 \, \text{cm} - 1.5 \, \text{cm} = 18.5 \, \text{cm}
