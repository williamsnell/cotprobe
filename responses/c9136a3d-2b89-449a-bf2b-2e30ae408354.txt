\[
\delta = 2 \arcsin\left(\frac{\sin\left(\frac{A}{2}\right)}{n}\right)
\]

Rearranging for \( n \):

\[
n = \frac{\sin\left(\frac{A}{2}\right)}{\sin\left(\frac{\delta}{2}\right)}
\]

### Step 1: Convert \( \delta \) to decimal degrees
\[
\delta = 32^\circ 40' = 32 + \frac{40}{60} = 32.6667^\circ
\]

### Step 2: Calculate \( \frac{A}{2} \) and \( \frac{\delta}{2} \)
\[
\frac{A}{2} = \frac{40}{2} = 20^\circ
\]
\[
\frac{\delta}{2} = \frac{32.6667}{2} = 16.3333^\circ
\]

### Step 3: Compute the sines

\sin(20^\circ) \approx 0.3420

\]

### Step 4: Solve for \( n \)
\[
n = \frac{\sin(20^\circ)}{\sin(16.3333^\circ)} = \frac{0.3420}{0.2817} \approx 1.7323
