\[
C = k_H \cdot P
\]

where:
- \( k_H \) is Henry's constant,
- \( P \) is the partial pressure of oxygen in the air.

\[

\[
C = k_H \cdot P_{\text{O}_2},

we calculate:

\[
C = (4.12 \times 10^9) \cdot (21278.25) \approx 8.77 \times 10^{13} \, \text{mol O}_2 / \text{mol solution}.
