To calculate the frequency of concordance, we focus on the pairs where at least one twin exhibits the trait (club feet). Concordance is the proportion of these pairs where both twins have the trait. 

Given:
- Concordant pairs (both affected): 13
- Discordant pairs (one affected, one unaffected): 20

The total number of relevant pairs is \( 13 + 20 = 33 \). The concordance is then calculated as:

\[
\text{Concordance} = \frac{\text{Concordant pairs}}{\text{Total relevant pairs}} = \frac{13}{33} \approx 0.3939 \, \text{or} \, 39.39\%.
\]
