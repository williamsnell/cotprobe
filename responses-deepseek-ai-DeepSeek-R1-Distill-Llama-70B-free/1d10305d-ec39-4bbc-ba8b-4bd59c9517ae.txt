This means the possible frequencies are \( f, 3f, 5f, \dots \), where \( f \) is the fundamental frequency.

The next higher harmonic after the fundamental is the third harmonic, which corresponds to \( 3f \). Substituting \( f = 131 \, \text{Hz} \), the frequency of the third harmonic is:

\[
