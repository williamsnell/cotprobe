\[
\lambda = \frac{h}{\sqrt{2mK}}
\]

- \( h \) is Planck's constant (\( 6.626 \times 10^{-34} \, \text{J·s} \)),
- \( m \) is the mass of the electron (\( 9.109 \times 10^{-31} \, \text{kg} \)),
- \( K \) is the kinetic energy in joules.

Rearranging for \( K \):

\[
K = \frac{h^2}{2m\lambda^2}
\]
