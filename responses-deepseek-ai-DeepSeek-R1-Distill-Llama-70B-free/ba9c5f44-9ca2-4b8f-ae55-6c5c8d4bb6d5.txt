On June 10, the painter assigned his right to receive payment to his nephew. The issue is whether this assignment is valid and whether the homeowner is obligated to pay the nephew.

However, the assignment here pertains only to the right to receive payment, not the duty to perform the services. This distinction is important because the duty to paint remains with the painter, and the assignment does not affect the performance of the work.

3. **Effect of the Anti-Assignment Clause**: The presence of the anti-assignment clause makes the assignment of payment rights invalid.
