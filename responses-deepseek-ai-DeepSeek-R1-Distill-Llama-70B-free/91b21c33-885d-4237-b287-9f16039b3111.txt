A qualified opinion is issued when the financial statements are fairly presented in all material respects except for a specific issue, such as a scope limitation or non-compliance with accounting principles. However, there are situations where a qualified opinion is not permissible, and instead, an adverse opinion or a disclaimer of opinion is required.
