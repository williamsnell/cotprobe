The ten percent rule of ecological efficiency states that only 10% of energy is transferred from one trophic level to the next, with the remaining 90% being lost to metabolic processes and other factors.

To determine how much energy a wolf receives from the original clover plants, we apply the ten percent rule at each step:

1. **Clover → Mice**: 10% of the clover's energy is transferred to the mice.  
2. **Mice → Skunks**: 10% of the mice's energy is transferred to the skunks.  
3. **Skunks → Wolves**: 10% of the skunks' energy is transferred to the wolves.

- Clover: 1000 calories  
- Mice: \( 1000 \times 0.1 = 100 \) calories  
- Skunks: \( 100 \times 0.1 = 10 \) calories  
- Wolves: \( 10 \times 0.1 = 1 \) calorie
