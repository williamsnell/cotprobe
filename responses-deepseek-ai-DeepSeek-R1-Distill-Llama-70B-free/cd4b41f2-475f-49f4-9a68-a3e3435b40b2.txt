For a reflecting surface, the radiation pressure \( P \) is given by:

\[
P = \frac{2S}{c}
\]

This accounts for the fact that reflecting surfaces experience twice the momentum transfer compared to absorbing surfaces. Substituting \( S = 1000 \, \text{W/m}^2 \) and \( c = 3 \times 10^8 \, \text{m/s} \):

\[
