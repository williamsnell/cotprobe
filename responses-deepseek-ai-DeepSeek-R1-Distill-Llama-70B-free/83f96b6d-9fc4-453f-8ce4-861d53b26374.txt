If the pressure \( P \) is constant during the process, \( \Delta(PV) \) simplifies to \( P\Delta V \), making the equation \( \Delta H = \Delta U + P\Delta V \). This holds true only when the pressure is constant. Therefore, the equation is applicable only for constant pressure processes.
